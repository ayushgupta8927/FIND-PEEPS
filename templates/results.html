A Flask Web-App for Face Detection

This Flask Web App functions as a fully-equipped Face-Detection software, with accurate Face and Feature Detection Mechanisms. The MTCNN Classifier has been utilised for the task of Face Detection. This has been paired with the capabilities of Python and Flask, inorder to achieve the highest possible efficiency and ease of use. The detected faces can be downloaded as a zip-file onto the client's device, for further reference and perusal.
